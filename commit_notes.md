### Architecture of Y$

- Relative Stability:
  - Anchored to the USD 
    - Chainlink Pricefeed
    - Set a function to exchange wETH and wBTC for USD equivalent
- Minting/Stability Mechanism:
  - Algorithmic/Decentralized
    - People can only mint the stableocin with enough collateral
- Collateral:
  - Exogenous (Crypto-collaterised)
    - wETH
    - wBTC
